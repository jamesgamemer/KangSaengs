Hendrickson

SR

Description

A Druid who now runs an apothecary. In the past, he served as a Grandmaster of the Holy Knights but was brainwashed by Fraudrin and acted to bring about the revival of the Demons. Since breaking free from that control, he has devoted himself tirelessly to atoning for his past mistakes.

Type: Dual Swords, Longsword, Lance
