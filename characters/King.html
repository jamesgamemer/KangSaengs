King

SSR

Description

A member of <The Seven Deadly Sins> bearing the mark of Sloth. He is also known as Harlequin, the king of the Fairies. After losing everything dear to him during the 700 years he spent away from the forest, he abandoned the name Harlequin and now lives as King, a member of <The Seven Deadly Sins>.

Type: Book, Wand, Staff
