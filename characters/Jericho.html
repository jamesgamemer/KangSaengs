Jericho

SSR

Description

She was once a Holy Knight of Liones and also served as the mentor to Lancelot, the child of Ban and Elaine. For reasons unknown, however, she became a Holy Knight of Camelot under King Arthur's rule and is now in open conflict with all of Liones.

Type: Dual Swords, Lance, Rapier
