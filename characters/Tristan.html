Tristan

SSR

Description

The son of Meliodas and Elizabeth, and the prince of the Kingdom of Liones. He admires and looks up to <The Seven Deadly Sins>. Though polite and cautious by nature, he will face any danger if it means protecting those he cares about.

Type: Longsword, Dual Swords, Greatsword
